Mobile application designed to help boat owners manage their boat's maintenance needs with ease. This app is designed for a technical service provider and offers a range of features to enhance the boat owner's experience. Users can enter their boat information, view real-time weather updates in their location, and book appointments with the technical service provider. The app also allows users to view the progress of their appointments and receive updates on their boat's repair status. Technical service staff can access appointment requests, make necessary adjustments, and communicate with boat owners about the situation and updates of the appointment. Additionally, the app allows staff to list users and view the information of each user with ease. With this mobile application, boat owners can enjoy a seamless maintenance experience, and technical service staff can efficiently manage appointment requests and communicate with boat owners to provide exceptional service.
